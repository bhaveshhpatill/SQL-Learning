## Business Concepts

- SQL retrieves data from tables.
- SELECT chooses which columns to display.
- WHERE filters rows.
- AND requires all conditions to be true.
- OR requires at least one condition to be true.
- ORDER BY sorts the results.
- ASC sorts in ascending order (default).
- DESC sorts in descending order.
- LIMIT restricts the number of rows returned.

## Business Thinking

Managers rarely ask for all data.

Instead they ask questions like:

- Show Mumbai customers.
- Show customers older than 25.
- Show the oldest 5 customers.
- Show only Name and City.

SQL is used to answer business questions, not just retrieve data.
